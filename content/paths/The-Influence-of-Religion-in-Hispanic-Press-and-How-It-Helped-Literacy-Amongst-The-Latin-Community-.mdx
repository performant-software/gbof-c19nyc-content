      immigrants, especially ones from impoverished areas, learned to read

      religious text, enhances their desire to continue to further the ability.\

## My path provides insight between the relationship between both religion and literature within early Hispanic-American communities. Religion is a very large aspect in Hispanic culture and the connection that it plays to early literacy rates, especially in farther removed country communities known as 'campos'. Due to this, its influence in the media is a large one, especially because this is what they turned to during times of hardship in being in a new country. The religion being in the media also fostered closer knit communities because of the messages in Catholicism / Christianity of unification that God had brought.
