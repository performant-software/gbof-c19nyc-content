Jose Martí and His Influence on Brooklyn Communities goes over the four Brooklyn locations Jose Martí had stayed when still in New York City. This path explores both questions of why had Jose Martí chose to stay in these Brooklyn locations when others were staying in boarding houses closer to the printing presses in Manhattan, and how had his influence in these Brooklyn communities help the latinx population grow with knowledge. By being able to understand the answer to these questions we can see how Jose Martí was able to influence the Latinx Brooklyn population whether it was through education or even an influx of more immigrants coming into Brooklyn through the years. We'll learn about the mark that Martí left on these communities and why he chose to stay in these communities in the first place.
