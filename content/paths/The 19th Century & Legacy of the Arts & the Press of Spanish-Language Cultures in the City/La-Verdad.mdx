La Verdad

Description

La Verdad is one of the many publications that could be found at 89 Nassau St. This Spanish language newspaper focused primarily on political and societal injustices that occured in both the United States and Cuba. As a result, Spanish speaking communities were kept updated and engaged while adjusting to this foreign world around them.

Historical Content

At the time many Latin Americans lived in exile and fear, these newspapers allowed an outlet for these quiet voices to be heard to a more broader audience. These newspapers played a role to provide as much information as possible, especially topics regarding inequalities.

Significance

This publication holds significance since it exposes political dilemmas while allowing diverse communities to connect ideas and their voice to be heard.
