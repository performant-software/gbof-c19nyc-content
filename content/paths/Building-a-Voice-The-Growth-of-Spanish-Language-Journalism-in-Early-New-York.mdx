This project, Building a Voice: The Growth of Spanish-Language Journalism in Early New York, explores how early Spanish-language publications in New York City grew out of religious, cultural, and intellectual spaces. Focusing on the influence of Félix Varela, it maps key sites that reveal the growth of early Spanish-language press
